## ISLAMIC VALUE
### Fair Queue System

THE APP ENSURES 'ADL' (FAIRNESS) BY USING A STRICT FIRST-COME, FIRST-SERVED DIGITAL QUEUE.

BECAUSE THE ORDERS GO STRAIGHT TO THE KITCHEN DASHBOARD, NOBODY CAN CUT THE LINE. EVERYONE GETS THEIR FOOD EXACTLY IN THE ORDER THEY PAID FOR IT.

[Image of mobile app mockup: Order Status screen highlighting the clear indicator for queuing] clear indicator for queuing

## ISLAMIC VALUE
### Hifz al-Mal (Protection of Wealth)

MANUAL NOTEBOOKS OFTEN CAUSE WRONG ORDER COLLECTIONS. A PAID ORDER IS AN AMANAH (TRUST) FOR THE CAFE OWNER. THE QR CODE ACTS AS A DIGITAL CHECK TO ENSURE THE EXACT PERSON GETS THEIR FOOD, PROTECTING THE STUDENT'S MONEY (MAL) AND FULFILLING THE TRUST.
