# USER PERSONA

**NAME:** RIDHWAN
**ARCHETYPE:** THE TIME-CRUNCHED STUDENT
**DEMOGRAPHICS:** 21 YEARS OLD, LIVES IN MAHALLAH (ON CAMPUS), FREQUENT USER OF LOCAL CAMPUS CAFES.

### Short Description
RIDHWAN IS A 2ND-YEAR STUDENT WITH A BACK-TO-BACK LECTURE SCHEDULE. HE RELIES ON CAMPUS STALLS FOR QUICK MEALS BETWEEN CLASSES BUT OFTEN FINDS HIMSELF FRUSTRATED BY LONG QUEUES OR ARRIVING ONLY TO FIND HIS FAVORITE DISH IS SOLD OUT.

### Needs
* TO GET A MEAL QUICKLY WITHOUT WASTING 20 MINUTES IN A QUEUE.
* TO KNOW IF A SPECIFIC STALL IS OPEN BEFORE WALKING ACROSS CAMPUS.
* TO ENSURE THE SPECIFIC ITEM HE WANTS (E.G., NASI LEMAK) IS STILL IN STOCK.

### Challenges
* WASTED TRIPS TO "MART" THAT ARE UNEXPECTEDLY CLOSED.
* ARRIVING AT A STALL ONLY TO REALIZE THE "SPECIAL" IS SOLD OUT.
* CURRENT DELIVERY APPS (GRAB/FOODPANDA) DON'T LIST THE SMALL VENDORS INSIDE THE CAMPUS.

# SELECTED SOLUTIONS

### SOLUTION 1: THE "REAL TIME STALL DASHBOARD"
* **FEATURES:** REAL TIME OPEN/CLOSED STATUS, LIVE QUEUE LENGTH AND ITEM AVAILABILITY.
* **IMPACT:** SOLVES THE "UNCERTAINTY" PROBLEM. STUDENTS WON'T WASTE TIME WALKING TO CLOSED OR SOLD-OUT STALLS.

### SOLUTION 2: QR & PICKUP NOTIFICATION
* **FEATURES:** STUDENTS CAN PRE-ORDER AND PAY VIA THE APP. THE APP SENDS A "READY FOR PICKUP" NOTIFICATION WHEN THE STALL OWNER MARKS THE ORDER AS COMPLETE.
* **IMPACT:** DIRECTLY ADDRESSES THE "LONG WAIT TIMES" AND THE LACK OF SUPPORT FOR SMALL VENDORS ON MAJOR DELIVERY APPS.

# WHY THESE TWO? (Justification)

### IMPACT ON STUDENT PRODUCTIVITY
Solves 2 biggest problems:
* Uncertainty
* Wasted Time

### LOW BARRIER TO ENTRY
Easy to implement:
* Does not need delivery system (like grab)

### FEASIBILITY FOR SMALL VENDORS
Affordable:
* Does not require specific hardware
* Can be operated using a mobile phone
