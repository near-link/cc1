## 1. CHOSEN APP

### GrabFood
**Problem:**
Grab only supports major vendors and "official" merchants. It completely ignores individual stalls in Mahallah cafes and local IIUM marts.

---

## KEY PROBLEMS

* LACK OF SUPPORT FOR SMALL CAMPUS STALLS
* NO INFO ON ITEM AVAILABILITY FOR MARTS
* NO REAL-TIME "OPEN/CLOSED" STATUS FOR LOCAL CAFE/MARTS
* NO QUEUE STATUS FOR STALLS

## PROBLEM STATEMENT

IIUM students need a way to verify stall status and pre-order meals from local campus cafes because current delivery apps like Grab do not support small campus stalls, leading to long wait times and uncertainty regarding food and item availability.
